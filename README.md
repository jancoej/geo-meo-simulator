MEO constellation visualization

The six MEO satellites are distributed across two orbital planes:

Plane A: MEO-1, MEO-3 and MEO-5

Plane B: MEO-2, MEO-4 and MEO-6

Each plane contains three satellites separated by 120 degrees. Plane B has a 60-degree longitude offset to improve visual and service diversity.

Satellite motion

Press START MOTION to animate the six MEO satellites along their two orbital planes. Press PAUSE MOTION to stop the animation. The GEO satellite remains fixed above its assigned longitude, consistent with geostationary operation.
